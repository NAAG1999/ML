ATMega32:
 
14 digital pins are supported 

one port

PWM are 6 pins (3,5,6,9,10,11)
 
A0-A5 :- for analog inputs

input value possible for the board is 7-12V

DC current per I/O pin - 20 mA

It has 16MHZ Quartz crsytal

1 ICSP header in circuit serial programming

It supports 3 types of memory:

1) Flash Memory : 32kb (32x1024 bytes)

	0.5 kb is reserved for the boot loader (not more than 0.5 kb)

2) 2 kb of Static RAM

3) 1 kb for EEPROM
